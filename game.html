    How to Play

    • You will roll two dice, e.g., 3 and 5.
    • Place these two numbers adjacent (like dominoes) on the 5×5 grid — horizontally or vertically.
    • You have 12 rolls total. If no placement is possible, you skip that roll.
    • After the game ends, each row and column is scored:
      • 2 in a row = 2 points
      • 3 in a row = 4 points
      • 4 in a row = 8 points
      • 5 in a row = 10 points
      • Multiple runs stack (e.g., 3-3-5-5-5 scores 2 + 4)
